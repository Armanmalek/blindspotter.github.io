The Blindspotter

To develop a reliable, laser sensor based device capable of detecting vehicles in areas outside of cyclist's field of view and display their position, thus increasing spatial awareness and preventing bicycle accidents.

 Find Out More

Let's make your next ride a safe one.

The Blindspotter is the next generation of safety technology designed to keep riders safe. This state of the art system allows the user to have 360 degrees of visibility without ever turning their head. This cutting edge technology is new to the industry and will aim to reduce the harm caused to bikers on roads globally.

Tech Specs

Sensors

Lidar-Lite V3 range finder identifies vehicles. Servo motor turns range finder over range. Arduino & Raspberry PI used to process and display information

Universal Compatibility

22.2, 25.4, 31.8 mm made to fit all industry standard sizings

Dimensions

Front Casing - H: 40mm W:110mm D:67mm

Back Casing - H: 80mm W:55mm D:104mm

Interface

Pulse width modulation (PWM) interface using external triggers. LCD monitor wired,
